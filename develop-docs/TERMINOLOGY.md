# Terminology

Glossary of terms used in the Sentry Cocoa SDK, with a focus on main-thread responsiveness.

## Apple Definitions

| Term                     | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           | Source                                                                                                                      |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Hang**                 | The main thread is unresponsive for 250 ms or more. Measured as the time between CFRunLoop states `.afterWaiting` and `.beforeWaiting`.                                                                                                              | [Apple: Understanding hangs in your app](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/xcode/understanding-hangs-in-your-app)   |
| **Severe hang**          | A hang lasting longer than the watchdog timeout, typically around 20 seconds on iOS. The OS terminates the app.                                                                                                                                      | [Apple: Addressing watchdog terminations](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/xcode/addressing-watchdog-terminations) |
| **Hitch**                | A frame that misses its VSYNC display deadline in the render pipeline. Measured as time past the expected frame presentation. This is not a runloop concept. Hitches are about the render pipeline, covering the commit, render, and display phases. | [Apple: Explore UI animation hitches and the render loop](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/tech-talks/10855/)        |
| **Commit hitch**         | A hitch caused by the main thread missing the commit deadline of around 8 to 16 ms depending on refresh rate. This is one subtype of hitch. The other subtypes (render and display hitches) happen out of process and cannot be observed by the SDK. | [Apple WWDC 2021: Demystify and eliminate hitches](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2021/10252/)                 |
| **Watchdog termination** | The OS kills an app that has been unresponsive for too long, typically around 20 seconds. Detected post-mortem by comparing app state across launches.                                                                                               | [Apple: Addressing watchdog terminations](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/xcode/addressing-watchdog-terminations) |

## Sentry Definitions

These are the terms used in the Sentry SDK and product. They are inspired by Apple definitions but adapted for Sentry error reporting.

### Hang Detection

| Term              | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Hang**          | The main thread is unresponsive, detected by measuring the time the runloop is blocked between `.afterWaiting` and `.beforeWaiting`. The duration is the total blocked time, including the per-frame budget. A 500 ms hang means the runloop was blocked for 500 ms. Reported as an issue with a sampled profile of the main thread during the hang. |
| **Major hang**    | A hang lasting more than 250 ms.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Severe hang**   | A hang lasting more than 500 ms.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Critical hang** | A hang lasting more than 1 second.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Fatal hang**    | A hang during which the app exits. Reported as a fatal exception rather than a handled one.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |

### Hitch Detection

| Term            | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Hitch**       | A runloop iteration that exceeds the frame deadline (~16 ms at 60 fps), measured only during interactive mode (touch input, scrolling). Unlike hangs, the duration is only the overshoot past the per-frame budget, not the total iteration time. A 20 ms runloop iteration at 60 fps is a 3.3 ms hitch. Hitches that cross the 250 ms threshold are tracked as hangs instead. Severity tiers based on overshoot: >10 ms, 5-10 ms, <5 ms. |
| **Hitch ratio** | The proportion of time, measured in milliseconds per second, that the app was showing a delayed frame during user interaction. Used to understand the severity of UI disruption across a session.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
